Tidy: named entity &apos; defined only in XML/XHTML

[edit]

Cause:

The apostrophe entity (&apos;) is defined only in XML and XHTML. This means that you cannot use it in regular HTML.

Also, some browsers do not support the &apos; entity.

[edit]

Example:

Bob&apos;s car
Bob&#39;s car
[edit]

Solution:

Replace "&apos;" with "&#39;".
